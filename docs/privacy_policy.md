# SubWatch Privacy Policy

Last updated: 31 March 2026

SubWatch is a private, on-device Android app that helps you review real paid subscriptions from SMS. It is built to be conservative: uncertain cases stay in Review instead of being over-confirmed, and bundled or included access stays separate from paid subscriptions.

## What SubWatch reads

SubWatch asks for SMS access only so it can check messages on this phone when you choose to scan. It does not continuously monitor your inbox in the background.

SubWatch may also ask for notification permission so renewal reminders can appear on this phone when you enable them.

## What SubWatch stores

SubWatch stores only local app data needed to show your subscription view on this phone. This can include:

- subscription summaries such as service names, amounts, and dates
- Review decisions and local labels
- subscriptions you add yourself
- reminder settings and reminder state

SubWatch is not designed to store your full raw SMS inbox as part of its app data model.

## What stays on your phone

Your messages are checked on-device. SubWatch does not require a cloud account to work.

SubWatch does not upload your SMS content, subscription summaries, reminders, or Review decisions to a SubWatch server.

## How SubWatch makes decisions

SubWatch is intentionally conservative. A payment is not automatically treated as a subscription. Setup messages, mandate messages, and Re 1 or Rs 2 verification charges are not treated as active paid subscriptions. Weaker recurring-looking cases can stay in Review until the evidence is clearer.

## Controls you have

You can:

- choose whether to grant SMS permission
- use the app without scanning by browsing the sample view first
- remove local app data from Settings
- report a problem through the support path in the app

If you clear all data from Settings, local summaries, Review decisions, reminders, and labels saved by the app on this phone are removed.

## Children

SubWatch is not intended for children.
